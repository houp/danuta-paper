Gdańsk School of Cellular Automata: Publications (2013–2025)

Below is a comprehensive bibliography of English-language publications (2013–2025) authored by members of the Gdańsk School of Cellular Automata — including Witold Bołt, Barbara Wolnik, Anna Nenca, Maciej Dziemiańczuk, Adam Dzedzej, Antoni Augustynowicz, Marcin Dembowski, Bartosz Makuracki, and Dominik Falkiewicz — focusing on cellular automata.

Each entry includes full citation metadata, short abstract summaries, research themes, and BibTeX snippets.

1. Analysis of Cellular Automata Dynamics

On the decomposition of stochastic cellular automata (2015)

Authors: Witold Bołt, Jan M. Baetens, Bernard De Baets
Venue: Journal of Computational Science, 11, 245–257.
DOI: 10.1016/j.jocs.2015.09.004
Summary: Introduces analytical tools for understanding stochastic CA by decomposing any such automaton into a mixture of deterministic rules. Enables detailed study of randomness in CA.
Themes: Stochastic CA Dynamics, CA Theory.

Synthesis of Research Lines
	1.	Identification and Learning: Innovative algorithms for identifying CA from partial data.
	2.	Task Solving: Continuous CA solving DCP and parity problems.
	3.	Number-Conservation: Structural theory and enumeration of NCCAs.
	4.	Non-uniformity: Exploration of heterogeneous CA rule spaces.
